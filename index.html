Privacy Policy for Away Timer

Last updated: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Away Timer ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data. All timer data (playlists, settings) is stored locally on your device using Android's internal storage. We do not have access to this data.

2. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions to function:

  • Notifications: To show the active timer countdown when the app is in the background.
  • Wake Lock: To ensure the timer continues running accurately when the screen is off.
  • Foreground Service: To play alert sounds and maintain timer state.

3. Payments

We use Google Play Billing for in-app purchases (Premium Upgrade). All payment transactions are handled securely by Google. We do not process or store your credit card information. Google provides us with an anonymous token to verify your purchase status.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children.
